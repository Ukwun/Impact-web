# Term-by-Term Curriculum Map (Senior Secondary and ImpactUni)

## Senior Secondary

Term 1:
- Venture design and customer problem framing
- Introductory financial logic and pricing decisions
- Live focus: participation confidence and team communication

Term 2:
- Projection worksheet execution and cash flow planning
- Governance and decision discipline
- Live focus: scenario decisions and evidence-based tradeoffs

Term 3:
- Investor simulation and pitch preparation
- Capstone business plan and presentation
- Live focus: presentation, questioning, and reflective iteration

## ImpactUni

Term 1:
- Personal finance, career capital, and opportunity mapping
- Problem validation and stakeholder discovery

Term 2:
- Venture execution systems and measurable milestones
- Practical modeling and resource planning

Term 3:
- Funding pathways and institutional credibility
- Team readiness and operating cadence

Term 4 (Applied Studio):
- Capstone delivery and showcase
- Recommendation pathways, partner introductions, and progression evidence

## Classroom Cadence

- Weekly live class: 75 minutes
- Monthly venture lab or studio checkpoint
- Quarterly pitch or showcase milestone

# Facilitator Operations Manual

## Roles in Session Delivery

- Lead Facilitator: teaches concept, drives outcomes
- Classroom Moderator: manages attendance, chat/Q&A, breakouts
- Mentor/Guest Expert: specialist input for advanced levels
- Programme Coordinator: monitors quality, replays, escalations

## Standard Session Sequence

1. Welcome and recap
2. Key concept teaching
3. Scenario, case, or activity
4. Learner participation and questions
5. Reflection and challenge prompt
6. Assignment briefing
7. Attendance confirmation and close

## Real-Time Operating Checklist

Before session:
- Verify meeting URL and learner list
- Preload facilitator notes and assignment reminder
- Confirm moderation and safeguarding escalation contact

During session:
- Mark attendance live
- Update current sequence step in the facilitation panel
- Track participation score indicators
- Capture incidents immediately with severity label

After session:
- Confirm attendance finalization
- Publish replay URL
- Issue assignment follow-up prompt
- Trigger progression review for at-risk learners

## Incident and Safeguarding Rules

- LOW: minor conduct issue, monitor only
- MEDIUM: repeated disruption or concern, coordinator notified
- HIGH: safeguarding threat, immediate escalation and session note lock

## Quality Targets

- Attendance capture completeness: >= 95%
- Replay publishing SLA: within 24 hours
- Facilitator progression review cadence: weekly
